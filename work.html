What is the Head of Publication design?

I lead a committee of designers who pull every element of the magazine into spreads you see in the final project. After the images have been shot and the articles are written, we put them all together as creatively as possible. As the head, I take everyone's individual designs and place them into the final copy of the magazine, add page numbers, make a table of contents, create the credits page, the cover and much more!

You must have extensive knowledge in Adobe InDesign in order to hold this position. That is how we make our spreads and pull everything together.
